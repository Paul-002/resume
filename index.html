About

I felt in love with programming since high school. The journey started with building a camera slider for timelapse, from parts "borrowed" from the then working printer. Printer hasn't worked since, but camera slider was way cooler. My satisfaction was directly proportional to the approval of my parents from another destroyed electronics.

As my career path at the university, I decided to go for a land surveying and cartography studies. During my four years-long journeys, I encountered many problems arising from technological backwardness. Unfinished projects, ideas from past and new aims pushed me back into where I started - improving lives by writing code.

Commercial projects

Ardentcode - Legal document finder

Large, scalable TypeScript application based on a monorepo structure, divided into multiple products following a microservices architecture to enhance modularity and ease of maintenance.

As a TypeScript developer, I was responsible for maintaining existing code, refactoring, and introducing enhancements to existing technical solutions. Also worked on new application features, including code development and cross-product integrations.

Furthermore, as part of my responsibilities, I was involved in process of managing app releases. Starting from release notes, went through the entire Continuous Delivery process, and culminated in releasing application on the production environment.

Participated in unit tests and worked on complementary E2E tests, enhancing the application's coverage for the areas or functionalities I was working on.

Technologies & tools used:

  • Custom framework based on the TypeScript programming language.
  • Unit testing and E2E test handled by Jest framework.
  • Declarative application routing used for managing the application state and handling asynchronous requests.
  • OAuth2 for user authentication security best practices to safeguard user data.
  • Project management, repositories, CI/CD based on Atlassian products (Jira, Bitbucket, Confluence, Trello).

Ardentcode - Training planner & performance tracker

Bi-directional athlete training planner application that facilitates effective communication and personalized exercise plans for athletes.

Coaches can easily prepare weekly training regimens tailored to their athletes goals and needs, while athletes can provide real-time performance data via workout apps.

Key features:

  • Customized training plans: Coaches can create individualized training plans for each athlete, optimizing their performance and progress.
  • Two-way communication: Coaches and athletes to exchange feedback, questions, and updates regarding training and performance.
  • Performance tracking: Athletes can log their training sessions, nutrition, and recovery data. Coaches analyze this information to make informed decisions about future training.
  • Data-Driven Insights: Coaches use real-time statistics to adjust training plans, ensuring continuous improvement and injury prevention.

Tech stack:

  • Backend & Database: Node.js + Express + MongoDB
  • Frontend: React + Storeon + CSS Modules
  • PWA: Offline access and a seamless mobile experience
  • Continuous Integration: Gitlab CI
