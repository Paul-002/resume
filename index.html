About

My first experience with AVR microcontrollers initiated my interest in programming. Unfortunately, for a long time I abandoned the ideas of further development in this field, treating them as a hobby. Over the years and the development of web applications, I have watched with interest the next new projects and the opportunities they bring. Huge sources of knowledge available on the Internet for free at your fingertips have had a positive impact on the decision to write your first application in vanilla.js. Undoubtedly, I consider it my success to return to my interests after so many years, which gave me a lot of fun. Along with the next lines of code, I began to discover new possibilities that libraries such as React.js, Vue.js and many other frameworks bring.

I'm looking for a job, in which I can develop myself professionally as a programmer. My main focus is on JavaScript language, his frameworks, and libraries.
